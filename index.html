History

Ancient Civilization: India is one of the oldest civilizations in the world, with evidence of human activity dating back to the Indus Valley Civilization around 2500 BCE. This period saw the development of sophisticated urban planning, trade, and culture. Vision

It emphasizes the coexistence of various cultures, languages, religions, and traditions within the country's borders. This vision promotes harmony and tolerance, celebrating differences while fostering a sense of national unity.
